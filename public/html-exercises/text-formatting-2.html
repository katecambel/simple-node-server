About Moi

Hi! My name is Katherine Anne T. Cambel, but you could simply call me Kate. I was born in September 10, 1995. I am 19 years old, almost 20 years old in just a few months. I was born in Paranaque City, Metro Manila. I lived in Manila for about 11 years, then we moved to Singapore and lived there for 4 years. Then we moved to Thailand and lived there for about a year. Then we moved back here to the Philippines. I've been living back here since. I love to dance, read, eat and sleep. I have watched around 20 television shows. I also love watching Formula 1.

Fun fact about me is that in the course of 4 years in high school, I studied in 3 schools because my mom's job kept moving us to places:
Freshman & Sophomore Years: Overseas Family School, Singapore;
Junior Year: International School Bangkok, Thailand;
Senior Year: International School Manila, Philippines
Some would question why I didn't study abroad for university, but even if I was accepted to universities in Canada, I chose to study locally. Therefore I am currently studying in University of the Philippines Los Banos, and taking up BA Communication Arts, majoring in Speech Communication. It might seem cliche and all, but even if I have read tons of books, my favorite book, or series per se, is still Harry Potter by J.K. Rowling. This is because it goes back to my childhood and it brought me to a whole different world in my imagination. It was my escape. In school, I have always remembered one of my favorite teachers telling me that making decisions doesn't have to be so hard unless you have set your priorities. Over time I found out he was right, and ever since, I have been keeping my priorities in check.
