3 Favorite Movies at the Moment

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

The book this movie was based on holds a special place in my heart and was the beginning of my journey with my love for literature. This is one of those rare times that I could say that the book was as good as the movie. Well, they were good in their own ways. The making of the movie was really creative especially the way they portrayed the animation of the wizardry and witchcraft happening around. Aside from that, Harry, being the main character and how little he was back then, had already posed as a promising actor that greatly affected the viewers especially me.

Pitch Perfect 2

Another challenge is laid upon the Barden Bellas as they embark on a new journey regaining the repect of their whole nation. The storyline fell short, but their singing voices and their puns are what kept me watching that movie over and over again. Furthermore, I just couldn't get enough of the Jessie and Becca tandem.

Mulan

People say I look a lot like Mulan from the Disney movie.
